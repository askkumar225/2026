Grilled Chicken

Grilled chicken is a healthy and delicious meal made by marinating chicken in spices and grilling it until juicy and golden.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken breasts
  • Olive oil
  • Lemon juice
  • Garlic, salt, pepper

Steps:

  1. Mix marinade ingredients.
  2. Soak chicken 1–2 hours.
  3. Grill 5–7 min per side.
  4. Serve hot with salad/rice.

Finger Chicken

Finger chicken are strips of boneless chicken coated in breadcrumbs and fried until crispy.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken strips
  • Corn flour / breadcrumbs
  • Egg, spices
  • Oil for frying

Steps:

  1. Season chicken strips.
  2. Dip in egg, coat with crumbs.
  3. Deep fry till golden.
  4. Serve with mayo/ketchup.

Tandoori Chicken

Spicy Indian grilled chicken marinated in yogurt & spices, cooked in a tandoor or oven.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken legs
  • Yogurt, lemon juice
  • Spices, mustard oil

Steps:

  1. Marinate chicken in yogurt + spices overnight.
  2. Grill/bake at 200°C for 30–40 mins.
  3. Serve with chutney.

Chicken Lollipop

A crunchy starter made from chicken wings shaped like lollipops, marinated and fried.

Ingredients:

  • Chicken lollipop pieces
  • Spices & sauces
  • Corn flour, maida, egg
  • Oil for deep fry

Steps:

  1. Marinate with spices & sauces.
  2. Coat with flour.
  3. Deep fry until crispy.
